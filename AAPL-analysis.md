## Background (Wikipedia Summary)
Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California. Founded in 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne, Apple designs, manufactures, and markets consumer electronics such as the iPhone, iPad, Mac computers, Apple Watch, and services including the App Store, Apple Music, and iCloud. It is one of the world's largest companies by market capitalization and revenue, known for its innovation in hardware, software, and services.
