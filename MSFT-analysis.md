## Background (Wikipedia Summary)
Microsoft Corporation is an American multinational technology megacorporation headquartered in Redmond, Washington. Founded in 1975 by Bill Gates and Paul Allen, it became a dominant force in the personal computer operating system market with MS‑DOS and Windows, and has since expanded into cloud computing, artificial intelligence, gaming, and a wide array of software and services. It is one of the world's largest companies by market capitalization and revenue, and a key member of the Big Tech group.
